Our Mission

Bridging Students & Employers

KFUEIT Job Fair is the flagship career event of Khwaja Fareed University of Engineering & Information Technology, Rahim Yar Khan. Now in its fifth year, the fair connects Pakistan's brightest engineering and IT graduates with top-tier employers from across the country.

The event features on-spot interviews, resume workshops, networking sessions, and panel discussions — all designed to give KFUEIT graduates a head start in their professional journey.

About KFUEIT

Khwaja Fareed University of Engineering & IT

Established in 2014, KFUEIT is a leading public sector university in Rahim Yar Khan, Punjab, Pakistan. The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Computer Science, and Natural Sciences — producing graduates who shape Pakistan's technological future.

  • Accredited by HEC Pakistan
  • State-of-the-art laboratories and facilities
  • Industry-focused curriculum
  • Strong alumni network across Pakistan and abroad
